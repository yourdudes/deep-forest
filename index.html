How We Work

From inquiry to delivery in four clear steps

A transparent, documented process keeps every party aligned — so you always know where your cargo stands.

Inquiry & offer

Share your commodity, grade, volume and destination. We respond with a firm offer and indicative terms.

Contract & finance

We agree specifications, Incoterms and payment — LC, DLC or documentary collection — and execute the contract.

Inspection & QC

Independent surveyors verify quality and quantity pre-shipment, with assays and certificates issued to contract.

Shipping & delivery

We book tonnage, manage documentation and track the vessel through to discharge at your nominated port.
