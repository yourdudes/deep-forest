About Deep Forest

A disciplined trading house for a moving world

We exist to make the movement of essential commodities simpler, safer and more transparent — connecting producers and end-users across continents with capital, logistics and trust.

Who We Are

Built on relationships, run on rigour

Deep Forest is an independent international trading company specialising in bulk commodities. We operate across four pillars — agricultural products, metals & minerals, energy, and industrial raw materials — sourcing from established origins and delivering to buyers in more than forty markets.

Our model is deliberately simple: be a counterparty buyers can trust. That means firm offers, contracts honoured to the letter, quality verified independently, and shipments managed end-to-end. We carry the financing, logistics and documentation complexity so our clients can focus on their own operations.

Mission & Vision

What drives Deep Forest

Our Mission

To move the world's essential raw materials reliably and responsibly — giving producers consistent demand and giving buyers consistent, contract-grade supply, wherever they operate.

Our Vision

To be the trading partner of choice for serious buyers across emerging and established markets — known for integrity, transparency and the discipline to deliver, cargo after cargo.

Our Values

The principles behind every trade

Bulk commodity trading is a business of trust at scale. These values keep us dependable when it matters most.

Integrity

We honour our contracts in full. A Deep Forest offer is one you can plan around, and a Deep Forest commitment is one we keep.

Transparency

Independent inspection, clear documentation and open communication. You see the assays, the origin and the status of your cargo.

Reliability

On grade, on time, on terms. Our logistics and finance discipline turns complex shipments into predictable deliveries.

Global perspective

We read the freight, currency and demand signals that move markets, and we put that perspective to work for our clients.

Responsibility

We pursue responsible sourcing and safe handling across every commodity class, and we work to reduce waste along the chain.

Partnership

We invest in long-term relationships with producers and buyers alike — repeat business is the truest measure of our service.

Our Story

From a single trade lane to a global desk

Deep Forest grew the way good trading houses do — one delivered cargo and one satisfied buyer at a time.

What began as a focused agricultural trade lane has become a diversified, multi-commodity operation spanning five continents — supported by trade finance, independent quality control and a logistics network that keeps cargo moving.

  • 2009

    Founded on agricultural trade

    Deep Forest opens its desk with a focus on grains and oilseeds between two key origins and destinations.

  • 2013

    Into metals & minerals

    Expansion into iron ore and base metals, adding independent assay and inspection partners.

  • 2017

    Energy desk launched

    Coal and refined products join the portfolio, with structured trade-finance facilities for larger lots.

  • 2021

    Industrial raw materials

    Chemicals, polymers and pulp & paper feedstock added to serve manufacturing clients directly.

  • Today

    A five-continent operation

    A diversified trading house moving millions of tonnes a year across forty-plus markets.
