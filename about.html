About Deep Forest

We carry the complicated part, so you can run your business.

Deep Forest is a Hong Kong trading desk for bulk commodities. We take on the sourcing, financing, inspection and shipping that make large cross-border trades hard — and hand you product that matches the contract, on time.

Who we are

One desk, four commodity classes, zero hand-offs you have to manage.

We trade across agriculture, metals & minerals, energy and industrial raw materials — and we deliver into more than forty markets. Whatever you source, you deal with the same team from first offer to final discharge.

Our promise is simple: be the counterparty you don't have to chase. That means firm offers you can plan around, contracts honoured to the letter, quality verified by independent inspectors, and shipments we own end to end. You focus on your operation; we'll handle the rest.

How we got here

We grew the way good trading houses do — one delivered cargo at a time.

No shortcuts, no overnight scale. What started as a single grain lane out of Hong Kong became a four-desk operation by earning the next order, then the one after that.

Today that lane is a diversified, multi-commodity desk spanning five continents — backed by trade finance, independent quality control and a logistics network that keeps cargo moving in any market.

  • 2009

    It started with grain

    We opened the desk in Hong Kong on a single mission: move grains and oilseeds reliably between two origins and two destinations.

  • 2013

    We added metals

    Iron ore and base metals joined the book, and we brought in independent assay and inspection partners to back every grade.

  • 2017

    The energy desk opened

    Coal and refined products came on, along with structured trade finance to support the larger lots buyers were asking for.

  • 2021

    We took on raw materials

    Chemicals, polymers and pulp & paper feedstock let us supply manufacturers directly, on schedules built for their lines.

  • Today

    Four desks, five continents

    A diversified desk moving millions of tonnes a year into forty-plus markets — still answering to the same standard we started with.
