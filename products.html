01

Agricultural commodities

Grain · Soybeans · Edible oils · Sugar · Coffee · Cotton

Feeding a mill, a crusher, a refinery or a food line? We pull grain and oilseeds, edible oils, raw and refined sugar, green coffee and cotton from established origins and land them where you need them — handling origination, quality and shipment so what arrives matches what you contracted.

Every lot ships with independent pre-shipment inspection and the certificates your market demands — moisture, protein, oil content, polarisation, grade. Quality is settled before the vessel sails, not argued over at discharge.

  • ✓Grain: wheat, corn, barley, sorghum & rice
  • ✓Oilseeds & oils: soybeans, soybean & sunflower oil, palm
  • ✓Sugar (ICUMSA 45 / raw), green coffee & cotton
  • ✓Bulk vessel or container; bagged or in-bulk, your call

02

Metals & minerals

Iron ore · Copper · Aluminium · Steel · Ferroalloys

If you run a mill, foundry, smelter or build at scale, you buy on chemistry — so we trade strictly to grade. Iron ore fines and lump, copper cathode, primary aluminium, finished steel and ferroalloys, every cargo backed by an independent assay and a spec you can hold us to.

Price follows grade, full stop. Chemistry and physicals are verified by recognised agencies before shipment, and the quality clause sits in the contract — so you never pay 62% money for 58% ore.

  • ✓Iron ore fines & lump (typical Fe 58–65%)
  • ✓Copper cathode (LME Grade A), aluminium ingot & billet
  • ✓Steel: HRC, rebar, wire rod, plate & sections
  • ✓Ferroalloys: ferro-silicon, ferro-manganese, ferro-chrome
Send us your metal specs →
03

Energy

Coal · Petroleum products · LNG · Biomass

Keep the plant fed and the lights on. We trade thermal and metallurgical coal, refined petroleum products, LNG and sustainable biomass — matched to the exact calorific, sulphur and spec your operation is built around.

Energy doesn't forgive late or off-spec. We coordinate inspection, calorific and sulphur testing and vessel scheduling so cargo lands inside your operating window — not a week after you needed it.

  • ✓Thermal coal (GAR 4200–6000 kcal/kg), met coal
  • ✓Refined products: gasoil, jet, fuel oil, bitumen
  • ✓LNG cargoes and term offtake structures
  • ✓Biomass: wood pellets & agricultural residues
Send us your energy specs →
04

Industrial raw materials

Chemicals · Polymers · Pulp & paper

A line that stops is a line that loses money. We keep manufacturers supplied with base and intermediate chemicals, commodity polymers and pulp & paper feedstock — delivered on schedules that respect just-in-time production.

For industrial buyers, consistency beats a one-off cheap lot. We supply against your technical data sheets, manage hazardous-goods documentation where it applies, and structure repeat-supply contracts that smooth your input costs over the year.

  • ✓Base chemicals: caustic soda, soda ash, methanol, urea
  • ✓Polymers: PP, PE, PVC & PET resins
  • ✓Pulp & paper: NBSK, BEK pulp, recovered paper grades
  • ✓Big bags, drums, ISO tanks or in-bulk per product
