Agricultural Commodities

Grains · Soybeans · Edible oils · Sugar · Coffee · Cotton

Deep Forest sources agricultural commodities from established producing regions and delivers them to mills, crushers, refiners and food manufacturers worldwide. We trade grains and oilseeds, edible oils, raw and refined sugar, green coffee and cotton — handling origination, quality verification and shipment so buyers receive product that matches contract.

Each lot is supported by independent pre-shipment inspection and the certificates your market requires — moisture, protein, oil content, polarisation, grade and more — so quality is never a question on arrival.

  • Grains: wheat, corn, barley, sorghum & rice
  • Oilseeds & oils: soybeans, soybean & sunflower oil, palm
  • Sugar (ICUMSA 45 / raw), green coffee & cotton
  • Bulk vessel or container; bagged or in-bulk on request

Metals & Minerals

Iron ore · Copper · Aluminium · Steel products · Ferroalloys

We supply mills, foundries, smelters and construction buyers with metals and minerals traded strictly by grade. From iron ore fines and lump to copper cathode, primary aluminium, finished steel products and ferroalloys, every cargo is backed by independent assays and clear specifications.

Assay transparency is central to how we trade. Chemistry and physical properties are verified by recognised inspection agencies before shipment, and quality clauses are written into every contract so price always reflects grade.

  • Iron ore fines & lump (typical Fe 58–65%)
  • Copper cathode (LME Grade A), aluminium ingot & billet
  • Steel: HRC, rebar, wire rod, plate & sections
  • Ferroalloys: ferro-silicon, ferro-manganese, ferro-chrome

Energy

Coal · Petroleum products · LNG · Biomass

Deep Forest trades energy commodities for power generation, industry and heat. Our portfolio spans thermal and metallurgical coal, refined petroleum products, LNG and sustainable biomass — matched to your calorific, sulphur and specification requirements.

Energy cargoes demand precision on quality and timing. We coordinate inspection, calorific and sulphur testing, and vessel scheduling so deliveries land within your operating window and meet the specifications your plant is built around.

  • Thermal coal (GAR 4200–6000 kcal/kg), met coal
  • Refined products: gasoil, jet, fuel oil, bitumen
  • LNG cargoes and term offtake structures
  • Biomass: wood pellets & agricultural residues
Enquire about energy →

Industrial Raw Materials

Chemicals · Polymers · Pulp & paper

We keep manufacturing lines supplied with dependable industrial feedstock. Deep Forest trades base and intermediate chemicals, commodity polymers and pulp & paper materials — delivered on schedules that respect just-in-time production.

For industrial buyers, consistency matters as much as price. We supply against agreed technical data sheets, manage hazardous-goods documentation where applicable, and structure repeat-supply contracts to smooth your input costs.

  • Base chemicals: caustic soda, soda ash, methanol, urea
  • Polymers: PP, PE, PVC & PET resins
  • Pulp & paper: NBSK, BEK pulp, recovered paper grades
  • Big bags, drums, ISO tanks or in-bulk per product
